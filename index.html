about me

A "born-again" dabler of all things web I prefer simplier designs and unclutteredness. I am a self-proclaimed #cleanfluencer and disciple of Marie Kondo's spark joy method. Things should spark joy and that include web design. (Psst... born again because I've rekindled my love of creating beautiful and useful web apps.)

A meditator at heart I want to try to live a life of #kindfulness in a world where kindness for all beings comes first. That includes using technology and the web in all sorts of ways to improve all our lives. Insight meditation gives me the opportunity to revisit my (design or otherwise) thoughts and helps me to focus on what is important. All useful skills for a web designer.

An art of tea practitioner I am a learner and teacher of tea for over 20 years. Through the art of tea, I have learnt the importance of patience and the smooth flow of things. I treasure the tea ceremony motto of ichigo-ichie "一 期 一 会" (lit. “for this time only")。 This is a concept of treasuring the unrepeatable nature of a moment. In web design, that is a very short moment where a user is either attracted to a web page or be turned away from it. A well-designed website is made for the user; not at the whims of the web designer.
